# TDR Vesting & Lockup Schedule

## Purpose
This document defines the public vesting and lockup commitments for TDR investors, validators, and ecosystem participants.

## Team & Advisors
- **Allocation:** 15% of initial supply
- **Cliff:** 12 months from TGE
- **Vesting:** 36 months linear after cliff
- **Custody:** Multisig custody with 2/3 signers
- **Transparency:** Monthly on-chain attestation of locked balances

## Community & Ecosystem
- **Allocation:** 35% of initial supply
- **Lockup:** 6 months partial lock for early contributors
- **Vesting:** 48 months linear release
- **Use:** Developer grants, bug bounties, liquidity mining, partnerships

## Treasury & Operations
- **Allocation:** 20% of initial supply
- **Lockup:** 12 months initial lock
- **Vesting:** 60 months linear release
- **Governing body:** Multisig + DAO vote after mainnet maturity

## Liquidity & Listings
- **Allocation:** 5% of initial supply
- **Lockup:** 12 months minimum for DEX/CEX liquidity
- **Use:** CEX listings, market maker operations, bridge reserves

## Staking Rewards
- **Allocation:** 25% of initial supply
- **Vesting:** Dynamic issuance via consensus
- **Lockup:** None; rewards unlock immediately

## Investor Protections
- No team sells before cliff + 12 months
- Treasury disbursement requires multisig approval
- Quarterly transparency reports published
- Audit attestations published on-chain
